🌿 Plant Hygiene & Disease Prevention

🧼 What is Plant Hygiene?

Keep crops, soil, and tools clean to prevent diseases.

🧹 Field Cleanliness

  • Remove infected plants
  • Clear weeds
  • Avoid stagnant water

🛠 Tool Hygiene

  • Clean tools regularly
  • Disinfect equipment

🌱 Seed Care

  • Use quality seeds
  • Maintain spacing

🐛 Pest Prevention

  • Inspect crops weekly
  • Use organic sprays

💡 Tips

  • Avoid overwatering
  • Maintain drainage
